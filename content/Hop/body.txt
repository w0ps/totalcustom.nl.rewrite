This site is built with my own CMS.<br>
It's designed to be easily managed by people with little technical knowledge.<br>
A webmaster can change the contents of his site just by adding folders, text files and images.<br>
For the more advanced: scripts and stylesheets are included as well.

<h3>Features</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Easily put texts and images on your site through a dropbox connection</li>
    <li>Include stylesheets (css/less) and javascripts just by putting them in the right folder
    <li>Automatic page refresh when logged on</li>
</ul>
